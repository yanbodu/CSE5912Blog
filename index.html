About The Game

wAR Games AR is a real-time strategy war simulator featuring augmented reality and developed
by Graduated Games in August 2018.

The game allows players to set up their battlefield in real-world environments. The players as commanders summon their massive legions to fight against AI or other players. The players need to compete for resources, train forces, upgrade their capabilities and eliminate all enemies.

The single-player mode allows players to experience the story of the game world and sharpen their skills. The multiplayer mode enables players to fight against each other locally or they might be able to obtain their ranks in a ranking system by defeating players throughout the world.

Game Mechanics

a. Races and unit type

The player can choose between two different races: Human and Skeleton, both have
three different types of unit which are tank, damage, and healer.

Tank/Hero

Visually bigger than other units, the strongest unit type
Health: 1000
Damage: 100
Damage range: 50
Purchase cost: $50 / 1 unit

Damage

Main damage unit type
Health: 300
Damage: 50
Damage range: 30
Purchase cost: $30 / 10 unit

Support

The most vulnerable unit type, have the ability to heal other units
Health: 150
Heal range: 30
Heal amount: 50 / time
Cooldown: 3 sec
Purchase cost: $40 / 1 unit
